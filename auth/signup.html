üìà The Tiers Explained

Tier 0: Type a bio. This will go somewhere safe until you're done with Tier 1a.

Tier 1a: 6 randomly-selected text-based questions about you. At the end of Tier 1a, your answers and your partner's answers will be exchanged. If you both vote to continue, you'll get to read each others' bios.

Tier 1b: 6 more randomly-selected text-based questions about you. At the end of Tier 1b, your answers and your partner's answers will be exchanged. If you both vote to continue, you'll have the opportunity to send a direct message for the first time. (Optional, 250 words/2000 characters maximum).

Tier 2: If Tier 1 was "let me tell you about myself", tier 2 has some "tell me what you've noticed about me" mixed in. At the end of Tier 2, you'll be given the opportunity to send a direct message (250 words/2000 characters max), and/or an image (which will be downgraded to match our pre-social-media aesthetic), and/or a voice message.

Tier 3: If Tier 1 was "This is me", and Tier 2 was "This is you", Tier 3 is more like "This is Us". After Tier 3, if you both vote to continue, you'll be given access to a chatroom where you can send as many direct messages as you like.

Chatroom: Stay as long as you like, or exchange contact info and ride off into your new adventures as friends together! You can send messages and a limited number of images and voice messages.

"You move at your own pace. No rush."
