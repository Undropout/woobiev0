Woobie isn't a dating app or hookup platform. There are plenty of those out there already.

This is a space for building genuine connections through shared vulnerability and trust. Whether you're looking to make a new friend or explore a deeper connection, Woobie creates a safe, intentional path to authentic human closeness.

You'll move through 3 tiers together, 12 questions each, sharing more of yourselves as you go. No pressure, no performance.

This is about two people getting to know each other at a pace that feels right.

The more you stick your neck out, the more your partner feels safe to stick their neck out, and you feel bonds of trust grow.

Take your time. Be yourself. See what happens.

üìà The Tiers Explained

Tier 0: Your Bio

Type a bio. This will be kept safe until you're done with Tier 1a.

Tier 1a: First Questions (6)

6 randomly-selected text-based questions about you. At the end of Tier 1a, your answers and your partner's answers will be exchanged. If you both vote to continue, you'll get to read each others' bios.

Tier 1b: Deeper Questions (6)

6 more randomly-selected text-based questions about you. At the end of Tier 1b, your answers and your partner's answers will be exchanged. If you both vote to continue, you'll have the opportunity to send a direct message for the first time. (Optional, 250 words/2000 characters maximum).

Tier 2: Creative Expression (12)

If Tier 1 was "let me tell you about myself", tier 2 has some "tell me what you've noticed about me" mixed in. At the end of Tier 2, you'll be given the opportunity to send a direct message (250 words/2000 characters max), and/or an image (which will be downgraded to match our pre-social-media aesthetic), and/or a voice message.

Tier 3: Building Together (12)

If Tier 1 was "This is me", and Tier 2 was "This is you", Tier 3 is more like "This is Us". After Tier 3, if you both vote to continue, you'll be given access to a chatroom where you can send as many direct messages as you like.

Chatroom: Your Space

Stay as long as you like, or exchange contact info and ride off into your new adventures as friends together! You can send messages and a limited number of images and voice messages.

"You move at your own pace. No rush."

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a dating app?

No. It's not like dating is bad; there are just billions of dating apps and don't come here looking for one because your partner probably isn't. This is an app where you learn to connect on a level no other app makes happen. This is a treatment for loneliness and to build a friendship with bonds of trust.

Can I match with multiple people?

Kinda. (in a future update, yes. While this is about building quality connections, not quantities, we understand if you're looking for more friends and there's nothing wrong with that; technically, that's why we all come to Woobie. For now, you can sign up with a different email if you'd like to pair with more than one person in a separate 1-on-1 friendship)

What if the match doesn't work out?

You can rematch. Either party can end the friendship when voting in-between tiers. No need to explain, no need to apologize, no need to assign blame. We'll let them know that there are billions of reasons another party would choose not to continue, the vast majority of which are not anyone's fault.

Is my data private?

Yes. We chose to found a nonprofit so no one could ever pressure us to do anything profitable or creepy with your data. No one but you and your partner knows anything about the two of you.
