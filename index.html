Woobie is a friendship-matching platform designed to help you make one genuine friend at a time.

No endless swiping. No ghosting. Just meaningful conversations with people who share your vibe.

How It Works

What is Woobie?

Woobie is an anti-loneliness platform focused on helping you make real friends, one person at a time. We match you based on shared interests and values, then guide you through meaningful conversations in stages called "tiers."

How does matching work?

After you sign up and create your profile (interests, dealbreakers, bio), we match you with one person who shares your interests and is looking for the same kind of connection. You both get a fun, anonymous "Woobie name" to start.

What are Tiers?

Tiers are conversation stages that help you get to know your match gradually:

Tier 1: Light questions about your personality and values
Tier 2: Deeper questions and creative prompts
Tier 3: Vulnerable sharing and emotional connection
Chatroom: Once you both decide to continue, you unlock a private chatroom to chat freely!

Is it anonymous?

Yes! You start with a fun Woobie name (like "Cosmic Gentle Phoenix"). You choose when—or if—you want to share your real identity or contact info. It's all about taking things at your own pace.

What if I don't click with my match?

At the end of each tier, you and your match both vote on whether to continue. If either person votes "no," the match ends respectfully, and you can get matched with someone new. No pressure, no awkwardness.

Is Woobie free?

Yes! Woobie is completely free to use. We're focused on building genuine connections, not making money off loneliness.

Who is Woobie for?

Woobie is for anyone looking to make a real friend—whether you're new to a city, feeling isolated, going through a life transition, or just want to meet someone who gets you. Friendship-focused, no dating pressure.
